Our Story & Mission

Since 2005, Hephzibah School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Silang, Cavite. Our name, meaning "My delight is in her," reflects our joy in nurturing each student's unique potential.

We believe in "Learning Through Play" - combining academic excellence with non-digital skill development to create well-rounded individuals ready for the challenges of tomorrow.
